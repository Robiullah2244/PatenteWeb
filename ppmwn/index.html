Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy outlines the type of information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

Information We Collect

When you register for an account on our website, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number. Small businesses may also link their Instagram and LinkedIn accounts during registration. Sound Boarding Coaches are required to register using only their LinkedIn account. In addition, we may collect information about your usage of the website, including your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit.

Use of Information

We use the information we collect to provide you with access to our services and to improve your experience on our website. Small businesses may use our chat functionality to receive advice from a Sound Boarding Coach. We may also use your information to send you updates and notifications about our services, and to provide customer support.

Security

We take security seriously and use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. We use SSL encryption to protect your data during transmission, and we store your data on secure servers with limited access.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services, such as payment processors or advertising partners, to provide you with our services. These services may collect your personal information in order to provide their services, and we will only share the minimum amount of information necessary to complete the transaction.
